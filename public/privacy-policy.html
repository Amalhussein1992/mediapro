Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 18, 2025

Welcome to Media Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our social media management application.

Important: By using Media Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you create an account:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, bio, preferences
  • Social Media Accounts: OAuth tokens for connected platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest)
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely via Stripe/PayPal)

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our app:

  • Posts created, scheduled, and published
  • Analytics and engagement metrics
  • App features accessed
  • Device information (OS, version, device type)
  • IP address and location (approximate)
  • Log data (crashes, errors, performance)

1.3 Content Data

  • Social media posts (text, images, videos)
  • Brand kits (logos, colors, fonts)
  • AI-generated content
  • Voice recordings (for voice-to-text features)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Provide and maintain our social media management services
  • Schedule and publish posts to your connected social media accounts
  • Generate analytics and insights
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions

2.2 AI Features

  • Generate content using OpenAI, Google Gemini, or Anthropic Claude
  • Create images using DALL-E
  • Transcribe voice to text using Whisper
  • Provide personalized content suggestions

2.3 Communication

  • Send you notifications about your posts and campaigns
  • Email updates about service changes
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

2.4 Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our app
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features

3. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • AI Providers: OpenAI, Google, Anthropic (for content generation)
  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal (for payment processing)
  • Cloud Services: AWS (for file storage)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Sentry (for error tracking)
  • Social Media Platforms: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc. (to publish your content)

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Prevent fraud or abuse
  • Respond to government requests

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Password Protection: Passwords hashed using bcrypt
  • Access Control: Role-based access to sensitive data
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and updates
  • Token Security: OAuth tokens encrypted at rest
Note: While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
